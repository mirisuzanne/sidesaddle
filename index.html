Riding SideSaddle* is a fragmented memory of our time together, and these bodies we inhabit — 250 interchangeable index cards following a cast of friends as they navigate fluid genders, relationships, and bodies that resist order, category, or completion. Based loosely on the life of Margaret Clap, and the myths of Hermaphroditus — SideSaddle* is an open-source novel published by SpringGun Press, released online as an OddBook, and performed with music by Teacup Gorilla.
